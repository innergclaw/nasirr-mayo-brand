The Journey

Building in the Shadows, Ready to Lead

I started building in 2015. Not for recognition — because I saw problems that needed solving. Behind the scenes, I taught myself web development, AI systems, and digital strategy.

For years, I networked quietly, built websites for local businesses, and refined my craft. No spotlight. Just consistent work.

Through SHOPNASGFX, I learned what it takes to build a service business — automated client intake, streamlined delivery, and systems that scale.

In 2021, I founded InnerG Intel to teach AI skills. In 2023, OwnYourWeb Systems to help businesses own their digital presence.

Now, after a decade of preparation, I'm stepping forward to lead the next wave of AI adoption.
