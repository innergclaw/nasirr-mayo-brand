The Journey

Building in the Shadows, Ready to Lead

I started building in 2015. Not for recognition — because I saw problems that needed solving. Behind the scenes, I taught myself web development, AI systems, and digital strategy.

For years, I networked quietly, built websites for local businesses, and refined my craft. No spotlight. Just consistent work and relentless improvement.

Through SHOPNASGFX, I learned what it takes to build a service business — automated client intake, streamlined project delivery, and systems that let me focus on creative work instead of admin. That foundation in entrepreneurship taught me to build businesses that scale without burning out.

In 2021, I founded InnerG Intel to teach AI skills to students and professionals. In 2023, OwnYourWeb Systems to help businesses own their digital presence.

Now, after a decade of preparation, I'm stepping forward. Not to chase fame — but to lead the next wave of AI adoption and digital transformation.
